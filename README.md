# 🌙 Hunt After Dark

> *One last game... that's all you wanted.*

## 📖 About

It's the middle of a scorching summer. Your parents have finally gone to sleep, and the house is completely silent. You quietly walk into the living room, turn on your old CRT television, connect your game console, and pick up the iconic light gun.

You just want to play one last round of **Pigeon Hunt**.

The screen flickers.

The game starts.

Then...

A strange knock echoes through the house.

At first, you ignore it.

Then it happens again.

This time, much closer.

Something is waiting in the darkness.

Armed with nothing but your camera, you must complete the game and survive the night before it's too late.

---

## ✨ Features

* 🎮 Inspired by the classic **Pigeon Hunt**
* 🎮 Minigame-based gameplay
* 👁️ Suspense-focused horror
* 🌑 Mysterious horror creature
* 🌘 Horror atmosphere
